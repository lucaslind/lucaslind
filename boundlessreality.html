Boundless Reality: Traveler Artistsâ€™ Landscapes of Latin America from the Patricia Phelps de Cisneros Collection
2015

An exhibition of Latin American traveler art of the 17th to 19th century at the Americas Society Gallery and the Leubsdorf Gallery at Hunter College. It addresses the importance of this art in its moment and unfolds the histories that surround the individual artists and artworks. Maps, archives, color shifts, and framing devices running through the exhibition communicate movement, and the void of an invisible horizontal datum represents the mystery of exploration.
