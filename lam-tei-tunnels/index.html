Lam Tei Tunnels
2014

“The most radical tactic will be not to engage in spectacular violence but to withdraw from the area of simulation, to disappear.” — Hakim Bey, T.A.Z

A project that attempts to visualize the power struggle between the hierarchical power of the state and the decentralized, rhizomatic logic of alternative societies in tunnels within the Hong Kong mountains.

Professor: Kazys Varnelis.
