to expose, to show, to demonstrate, to inform, to offer: Artistic Practices around 1990
2015

An exhibition at the mumok Museum in Vienna surveying critical art practice in the late ’80s and early ’90s and featuring works by more than fifty artists and artists’ groups. The exhibition graphics embody a site-specific approach to placing text and image in space, taking advantage of the unusual architectural restraints and opportunities afforded by the mumok building.
