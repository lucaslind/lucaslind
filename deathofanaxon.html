Death of an Axon
2013

A proposal for a monument to the axon consisting of a drawing of a chronological record of the axonometric type and an object which combines 13 iconic projects in space. Similar to Eisenmanâ€™s House X representational model, the object reflects the axonometric transformations on the projects. The monument represents various allegiances including Corbusian modernism, utopian-leftist-rationalism, theoretical and philosophical postmodernism, and deconstructivsm.

Professor: Sjrdan Weiss.
