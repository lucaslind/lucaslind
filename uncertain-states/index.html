Uncertain States of La Festa
2014

In order to offer an answer to the debate underlining the stagnation of Barcelona’s brand concerning club culture, the project proposes spaces that trigger collective experiences and the creation of another self in relation to others. Barcelona’s once vibrant social scene has become increasingly homogenized, catering to the large number of tourists visiting the city. The redesign of the city’s social scene and economies presents an opportunity for all parties – government, citizens and tourists – to contribute to creative economic growth through collective invention and participation.

Professor: Cristina Goberna.
