Uncertain States of La Festa
2014

At dance clubs, communication occurs not only through language, but also through bodily movement in space in relation to sound and light. In order to offer an answer to the debate underlining the stagnation of Barcelona’s brand concerning club culture, the project proposes a space in which sounds and lights trigger a collective experience and the creation of another self in relation to others.

Professor: Cristina Goberna.
